Some books I Recommend

  • Thinking, Fast and Slow by Daniel Kahneman

    A fascinating exploration of how our minds work, focusing on the two systems of thinking: fast, intuitive thoughts and slow, deliberate reasoning.

  • Fooled by Randomness: The Hidden Role of Chance in Life and in the Markets by Nassim Nicholas Taleb

    A brilliant take on the unpredictable nature of life and how randomness shapes our world and perceptions.
